Privacy Policy

At Code Smiles, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This page explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the details you provide when using our website and booking services.

Information We Collect

We may collect your full name, email address, phone number, age, preferred schedule, and any additional notes you provide when submitting a booking request.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to review appointment requests, contact you regarding your booking, respond to concerns, and improve our website and services.

Appointment Requests

Submitting a request through our website does not automatically confirm an appointment. All requests are still reviewed by our staff before approval.

Data Protection

We apply reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure while it is handled through our system.

Your Rights

You may request correction of inaccurate details or ask questions about the information you submitted through our website.

Contact & Updates

If you have privacy concerns, you may contact us through our website. This policy may also be updated from time to time when needed.
