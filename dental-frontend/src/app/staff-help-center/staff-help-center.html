Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026  Â·  Code Smiles Dental Clinic

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide when using the Code Smiles patient portal, including:

  • Full name, email address, phone number, and date of birth
  • Dental and medical records, X-rays, and treatment history
  • Appointment details, booking requests, and scheduling preferences
  • Insurance information and billing records
  • Communication preferences and notification settings

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and manage dental care services and appointments
  • Send appointment confirmations, reminders, and updates
  • Process insurance claims and billing
  • Improve your portal experience and clinic services
  • Comply with applicable healthcare laws and regulations

3. How We Protect Your Information

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure, access-controlled database storage
  • Role-based access controls for clinic staff
  • Regular security reviews and updates

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share it only with:

  • Authorized clinic staff involved in your care
  • Insurance providers for claims processing (with your consent)
  • Regulatory authorities when required by law

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information stored in the portal
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or outdated information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@codesmiles.com.

6. HIPAA Compliance

Code Smiles adheres to the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). Your protected health information (PHI) is handled in accordance with HIPAA Privacy and Security Rules.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our Privacy Officer at privacy@codesmiles.com or call (555) 123-4567.
